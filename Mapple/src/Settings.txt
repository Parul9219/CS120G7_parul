In this screen user will be able to make change in different Categories of settings.

In General settings we will have option of Language selection to choose from supported languages default will be English

In Audio settings we will have Sound Effects, Ambient Sound, Music Volume under Volume controls and Mute All options to let user select

In Gameplay Settings we will have Difficulty Level(Easy , Normal , Hard) to user select one of them

In Account Settings under which we will have Profile Customization, Privacy settings, Social Settings(to share the result over social media)
