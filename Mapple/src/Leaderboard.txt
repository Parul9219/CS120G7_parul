The Leaderboard section of the game will store and display data from all
users of the game that are logged into an account and store their play data.
The Leaderboard will allow users to view the scores of all other users with the
user with the lowest average score ranking at the top and the highest at the bottom.
The user will also be able to filter the leaderboard for different continents e.g.
they will be able to filter for Europe and only european users will show on the
Leaderboard. Also, the user should be able to click a button and find where they sit
on the Leaderboard.
The Leaderboard will display the position the users sit on the Leaderboard, their
username and their average score.
